Our Services

Work Modes

Support agents spend a lot of time trying to identify what issues need their attention first. To manage customer complaints effectively, they need a system that streamlines and organizes these complaints. Work Modes from Zoho Desk sorts complaints based on time due, priority, and status, automatically. This helps agents save time and decide which ticket they need to attend immediately.

Sentiment Analysis

We Organizes complaints according to the sentiment analysis of the ticket. Zia, the AI assistant, analyzes the ticket for signs of the customer's feelings, flags tickets into Positive, Negative' and Neutral categories. Agents can attend to the tickets with a negative sentiment first to ensure a quick response and reassure upset customers.

Auto Suggest

Effective customer complaint management requires quick responses. Zia, the AI assistant, pulls relevant articles from your KB and has an answer prepared even before your agents read the ticket. All they need to do is hit send. This ensures consistently faster and more accurate responses.

What Are Types Of Complaints?

Academic & Non-Academic

Academic or non-academic areas of dissatisfaction with instructional or support services. Examples of academic complaints include a disputed course grade, faculty performance, or course information.
Examples of non-academic complaints include a disputed late registration fee, delivery of support services, or student conduct.

Sexual Harassment

Inappropriate introduction of sexual activities or comments into the workplace or learning environment.
While sexual harassment may involve relationships among equals, it often involves relationships of unequal power, giving rise to elements of coercion centered on sexual activity for opportunities of benefit such as improved job or academic status.

Discrimination

Discrimination is the unfair treatment of people and groups based on some characteristics such as race, gender, age or sexual orientation.Difference in treatment in any service, program, course or facility on the basis of race, color, creed, gender,
religion, sex, national origin, disability, ancestry, age, sexual orientation, pregnancy, marital status, or parental status.

Grievance

An unresolved complaint shall be termed a grievance.
A student who submits a complaint to the appropriate College officials and who is not satisfied with the plan of resolution.

Public Services

Public services complaints often revolve around issues related to government and public-sector organizations.
Citizens may encounter various challenges or concerns when interacting with government services or entities.Administrative Issues: Delays in processing applications or paperwork. Confusion or errors in official documentation. Difficulty obtaining necessary permits or licenses.

Educational Institutions

Educational institutions play a crucial role in the development and success of individuals, and addressing complaints in a timely and effective manner is essential for
maintaining a positive learning environment. A robust complaint management system within educational institutions can help resolve issues, enhance the overall educational experience, and contribute to continuous improvement in the institution's policies and practices.Academic disputes. Faculty or staff behavior concerns. Facilities and infrastructure problems.

Health & Safety concerns

Health and safety concerns complaints typically involve issues related to the well-being and protection of individuals within a particular environment, whether it be a workplace, public space, or another setting.Unsafe Working Conditions: Complaints about hazards in the workplace, such as inadequate safety equipment or poorly maintained machinery.

Facility/Location issues

Facility/location issue complaints typically involve problems related to the physical infrastructure and surroundings of a specific place. Whether it's a commercial building, public space, or residential area, these complaints address concerns that impact the usability, safety, and overall experience of the location.Wi-Fi and Technology Issues: Concerns about unreliable or inadequate internet and technology infrastructure. Complaints related to poor connectivity or outdated technology.
